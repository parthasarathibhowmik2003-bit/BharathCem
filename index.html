Our Legacy

From a single
kiln to a nationwide
legacy

Founded in 1958 in Rajasthan, BharathCem began as a modest cement plant with a vision to support India's growing infrastructure needs. Today, we operate 12 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities across 8 states, producing over 40 million tonnes of cement annually.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has made us the preferred choice for India's most ambitious construction projects — from the Mumbai Coastal Road to the Delhi-Mumbai Expressway.
